## FAQ

**What is the Pluto in Libra generation's defining project?**
The complete structural reconstruction of the systems that produce inequity — not just removing the most obvious injustices, but dismantling the structural arrangements that generate inequitable outcomes in the first place.

**How does this generation approach relationships?**
With the insistence that genuine partnership requires genuine equality — that relationships must be built on authentic mutual respect rather than inherited convention or power differential.

**What are the gifts of this generational placement?**
Sophisticated understanding of structural injustice, genuine commitment to fairness that goes beyond surface-level compliance, and the philosophical framework for understanding how systems rather than individuals produce inequitable outcomes.

**What are the shadows?**
The reduction of all relationship to power analysis, the inability to appreciate genuine goodwill within structurally imperfect situations, and the perfectionist application of justice frameworks that leaves no room for genuine relationship complexity.

**How does Pluto in Libra affect cultural aesthetics?**
With the complete reconstruction of what is considered beautiful and whose aesthetic expressions are legitimate — the expansion of the aesthetic canon to include what was previously excluded.
